PROJECT

Job Ad Poster

An illustrated job ad poster for Dice205 Digital with the theme of "reaching new heights".

The goal of the illustration was to entice veteran developers in the tech industry to apply to the agency by representing those who worked in a corporate environment as employees going through an endless climb and contrasting it with the imagery of being rescued by a hot air balloon filled with supportive individuals to represent the opportunity to work for an uplifting and fast-growing startup.

Two applicants with a combined experience of 12 years were rescued from their mundane corporate jobs.
