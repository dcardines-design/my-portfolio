PROJECT

JFC eValues

Jollibee Food Corporation (JFC) is the largest fast food chain in the Philippines with over 6 brands and 1,150 stores nationwide.

The project was a learning management system which aimed to improve the company’s on-boarding process by digitizing their values training material, adding interactive games, and establishing an exam feature to ensure that applicants-in-training know JFC’s values inside and out.

I worked as the lead designer and Adobe Captivate developer, together with 3 other supporting designers. Design and development lasted for 5 months and the project was in its final phase before it was discontinued due to reasons that I can't disclose (i.e., NDA). Adobe Captivate was used to develop the whole platform.

I'm really proud of the work we've done with this even though it didn't see the light of day.
