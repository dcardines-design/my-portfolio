PROJECT

JGF Annual Report

Jollibee Group Foundation (JGF) is the corporate social responsibility arm of the Jollibee Food Corporation. The foundation implements programs on agriculture, disaster-response, education, and
school-feeding progams.

The brief was to design and develop a website for their foundation's 2019 annual report. The project's two main goals were to increase brand appeal by spreading the impact of their programs to consumers and to digitize their annual report which was previously in print form.
