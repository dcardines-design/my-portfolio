PROJECT

Lulla

Lulla is conceptual project for an anxiety management and social media platform for pregnant women.

Research shows that social and (immediate) professional support helps ease stress and anxiety among pregnant women. If these are readily available and combined with meditation exercises guided by data-driven HRV (or “stress”) sensors (as seen in some Apple Watch applications), a platform like this could benefit expecting mothers throughout their gestation.
