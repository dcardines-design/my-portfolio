PROJECT

Escuela Taller Safety Icons

Escuela Taller de Filipinas Foundation, Inc. is a training center based in Intramuros, the historic walled city of Manila. Their mission is to uplift out-of-school youth from poverty by teaching them skills to preserve heritage structures such as churches and very (very) old houses.

This was part of an outreach program, led by our former university professors, that aimed to help local NGOs with their organizational communication. These icons belonged to an integrated safety communication project with the purpose of improving the NGOs safety record. Along with these icons, safety posters, videos, and PowerPoint presentations were produced to serve as visual aids to promote overall organizational safety.
